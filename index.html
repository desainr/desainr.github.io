About

Hello, thank you for visiting my website! I am a recent graduate from Miami University, having just completed a Bachelor of Science in Statistics with a minor in Computer Science.

I am proudly from the Detroit area and despite being a lifelong Michigan Wolverines fan, I have just begun my career in Columbus, Ohio. I am currently an Associate Consultant at HMB, an IT consulting company based in Columbus.

I primarily serve as a .NET developer working to build custom web applications, but have been involved in all many other aspects of web development using a variety of technologies.

Alongside my career in software development, I have an interest in the world of big data. I hope to one day combine my experience in programming and statistical education into a career in data science.

Every project or problem that I come across presents an opportunity to learn. I strive to challenge myself and to improve my technical and professional abilities by working on small personal projects, learning from others, and staying curious!
